Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ for Opticians

What sets FAVR apart from other platforms?

FAVR is the only platform where customers can specifically search for opticians by brand. As an optician, you present yourself in a premium environment with top brands and customers actively seeking high-quality eyewear.

Why is it important to have a profile on FAVR?

Products from over 100 premium brands and 26,000 opticians – in 7 languages – form the backbone of FAVR. Google rewards FAVR's consistent focus on user interest with top rankings. FAVR is already the world's most visited website in the premium eyewear segment.

Does registration on FAVR cost anything?

No – if your store is already in our database, registration is free. If not, there is a one-time setup fee of €49. Check here.

How can I integrate brand content into my own website?

A Logo Wall can be integrated free by any registered optician. The fully automated integration of brand profiles, product photos, videos and news is reserved for PREMIUM PRO.

Which brands are available?

Logos and a brand profile are available for every brand. If you miss a brand, just let us know – we'll add it for you. In-depth content (photos, videos, news, products) currently exists for approximately 100 brands.

How does the content on my website stay current?

Your Smart Content Modules automatically sync with the FAVR server. Whenever we update photos, videos, logos or news, these are automatically delivered to your modules.

Do all optician websites with Smart Content Modules look the same?

No, for four reasons: 1. You have the content of your individual brands. 2. The font adapts to your layout. 3. Each optician embeds modules where they fit best. 4. By default, the latest content is always synced – or you can randomize or manually select specific content.

Do I need to relaunch my website for the Smart Content Modules?

No, the modules can be inserted into almost any existing system. We're happy to check this for you.

What are the contract terms for Premium packages?

With credit card/PayPal: monthly term, cancel any time. With invoice: annual term, cancel by end of year. Always with 1 free month at the start.

Can I change my package at any time?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time – when upgrading to PREMIUM PRO, Smart Content Modules are available immediately.

Do you also offer complete website redesigns?

Yes. At smartopticianwebsites.com we offer complete new design and development of optician websites – including SEO optimization and content service.
