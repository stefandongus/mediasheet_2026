Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ for Opticians

What distinguishes FAVR from other platforms?

FAVR is the only platform where customers can specifically search for opticians for particular brands. As an optician, you present yourself in a premium environment with top brands and a clientele that specifically seeks high-quality eyewear and opticians.

Why is it important to have a profile on FAVR?

Products from over 100 premium brands and 26,000 opticians – in 7 languages – form the foundation of FAVR. FAVR's consistent focus on user interest is rewarded by Google with top rankings. FAVR is already the world's most visited website in the premium eyewear segment.

Does registration on FAVR cost anything?

Nein – sofern dein Geschäft bereits in unserer Datenbank gelistet ist, ist die Registrierung kostenlos. Ist dein Geschäft noch nicht bei FAVR gelistet, fällt eine einmalige Einrichtungsgebühr von 49,– € an. Hier prüfen.

How can I integrate brand content into my website?

Any registered optician can integrate a Logo Wall for free. The fully automated integration of brand profiles, product photos, videos and news is reserved for PREMIUM PRO.

Which brands are available?

Logos and a brand profile are available for every brand. If you're missing a brand, just let us know – we'll add it for you. In-depth content (photos, videos, news, products) currently exists for approximately 100 brands.

How does the content on my website stay current?

Your Smart Content Modules are automatically synchronised with the FAVR server. As soon as we update photos, videos, logos or news, these are automatically delivered to your modules.

Do all optician websites with Smart Content Modules look the same?

No, for four reasons: 1. You have the content of your individual brands. 2. The font adapts to your layout. 3. Each optician integrates the modules at the most suitable location. 4. By default, the latest content is always synchronised – or you can use randomisation or specifically select certain content.

Do I need a website relaunch for the Smart Content Modules?

No, the modules can be integrated into almost any existing system. We're happy to check this for you.

What are the terms of the Premium packages?

With credit card/PayPal: monthly term, cancellable monthly. With invoice: annual term, cancellable until end of year. Each with 1 month free at the start.

Can I change my package at any time?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade possible at any time – when upgrading to PREMIUM PRO, the Smart Content Modules are immediately available to you.

Do you also offer complete website creation?

Yes. At smartopticianwebsites.com we offer the complete redesign of optician websites – including SEO optimisation and content service.
